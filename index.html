Frequently Asked Questions

What is Endgame?

Endgame is a game I have been taught by my English teacher.

How do I play?

The game is fairly simple. The website generates a word for you and you have to continue with a word that starts with the letter that the website's generated word ends with. Then, the website will play with you and will give you a word that starts with the letter that your given word ends with.

What are these words?

Yes, these words may be a little bit complex and unfamiliar. This is due to the word list containing more than 350,000 words, so some of them will be Middle English.
